I felt like I should read everything written about making illustrations for tech companies, I think I read most of what I could find on the internet and here on medium, of course, Meg Robichaud is one of the medium writers that stood out, read on how other companies have used illustrations, how their process was like. 
Slack, Airbnb, Shopify, medium, how they have used illustrations, in hopes that I will get some “Must avoid lists” or something. I did not get any list rather, that led me to read more, that made me curious to understand why people used illustrations in the first place. But anyways I made a list of companies that used them and wrote down what is the purpose they are serving. Since the modern UIs have gradients and curved Buttons, no borders on everything(in contrast to the Windows 98).
